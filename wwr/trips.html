Featured Trips

Beginner

Ruby Canyon Float

Drift through the stunning red-rock scenery of Ruby Canyon on this relaxing half-day float. Perfect for families with young children, first-timers, or anyone who wants to soak in the beauty of the Colorado River without intense rapids. Wildlife sightings are common along this stretch.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Difficulty: Class I–II
  • Age Minimum: 5 years
  • Price: $65 per person
Intermediate

Westwater Canyon Full Day

Westwater Canyon is one of the premier day trips in the American West. This full-day adventure takes you through a series of exciting Class III rapids set inside a dramatic black rock gorge. Includes a riverside lunch and plenty of time to swim in the calmer stretches.

  • Duration: Full day (8 hours)
  • Difficulty: Class III
  • Age Minimum: 12 years
  • Price: $120 per person
Advanced

Grand Canyon Expedition

The ultimate Colorado River experience. This multi-day expedition takes you deep into the Grand Canyon, tackling legendary Class IV rapids including Lava Falls and Crystal Rapid. Our expert guides and fully-catered riverside camps make this a truly once-in-a-lifetime journey.

  • Duration: 5 days / 4 nights
  • Difficulty: Class III–IV
  • Age Minimum: 16 years
  • Price: $1,200 per person
