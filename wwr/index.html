World-Class Rapids

The Colorado River carves through some of the most dramatic canyon scenery in North America. Our expert guides lead you through legendary stretches of white water, including sections of the Grand Canyon and Ruby Canyon right here near Grand Junction.

Safety You Can Trust

Every trip is led by certified, highly experienced guides trained in swift-water rescue. We provide all safety equipment, conduct thorough pre-trip briefings, and maintain a perfect safety record. Your adventure starts with confidence.

Trips for Every Level

Whether you are bringing the whole family on a calm scenic float, planning a corporate team outing, or chasing the thrill of serious rapids with friends, we customize the experience for your group. No experience necessary — just a sense of adventure.
